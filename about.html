About Us

DELNAAZ JOURNEY

Delnaaz is a premium beauty salon for men and women who desire to look the best every day.

Right from day one, when Neetu Shinde started Delnaaz 20 years back, the only aim was to be the best in the business! Starting from a very humble background and today standing at the top of the game, the success story though complete still goes on with the same passion and fire that was there 20 years back. In an industry where success is measured by the number of happy clients you have, Delnaaz has always strived to be one step ahead!

According to Neetu Shinde, the biggest success for her has been educating and training more than 3000 students to date who now themselves are successful entrepreneurs and run their businesses in the beauty field. Along with a very large base of happy clients she has developed and nurtured so many young aspiring women to become proud individuals who can take on the world by themselves!

We make sure that all the services provided at our salons meet the international standards.

Through our advice and solutions from the expertise in this array, we aim at giving the best services through our state-of-art facilities. Our professional stylists and beauty experts are constantly updated with the latest trends and fashion advices that help them to work efficiently and deliver outstanding results!

Give us an opportunity to serve you once, we are sure you'll love to come back to us again and be our esteemed customer forever.ost confidence and Delnaaz would take pride in being a part of it. .
